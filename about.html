My name is Billie. I grew up in Honolulu, Hawaii and recently graduated from Northeastern University in Boston with a degree in Biology and a minor in Interaction Design. Employing my multi-disciplinary background, I approach design with both empathy for the user and a data-driven mindset. I'm motivated by my curiousity about the intersection of the physical + digital and how design thinking can tackle complex problems.

I'm currently working on accessible healthcare @ Vien Health. Outside of designing, you can find me on a road trip, in an art museum, in my kitchen baking bread, or attempting to teach myself bass guitar.
