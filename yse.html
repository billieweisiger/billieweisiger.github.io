Site redesign

Improving usability to increase metrics for a non-profit.

THE PROBLEM

Youth Society for Education, YSE, is a non-profit working to provide educational resources for underprivileged students. YSE reached out to UX Rescue for help with their website, which serves their branches in both Myanmar and the US, with the hope of increasing volunteer interest and donations.

OVERVIEW

Team
3 designers

1 PM

Goal
The aim of this redesign is to help increase volunteer interest and donations.

IMPACT

This project was unfortunately put on hold before implementation due to issues in Myanmar at the time. While the site isn't live, our team delivered a high fidelity prototype of the redesigned website as well as refined design standards to stakeholders.
