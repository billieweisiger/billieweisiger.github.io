Interview Scheduling

Self-scheduling experience for job candidates.

THE PROBLEM

Before getting placed on an assignment through EmployBridge, job candidates must complete an interview with a recruiter. It was found that the current interview scheduling experience has a high no-show rate.

16% of the no-show reasons were potentially avoidable
Research found that people often forgot about their interviews or had miscommunication from EB.

33% of users wanted to reschedule but had trouble when they tried
Improving the rescheduling experience could potentially fix a pain point our users are facing and decrease our interview no-show rate.

Business Goal
The goal with this new experience is to increase interview show rate by 15% (+900 interviews per week).

IMPACT

This work has shipped, is currently live in 5 EB branches, and is continuing to roll out. The business goal of this streamlined scheduling experience is to increase interview show rate by 15%. Data is still being collected on that, but early success indicators show that interviews scheduled have increased 10% and 92% of candidates that create an account go on to schedule an interview.
